Privacy policy
LAST UPDATED · APRIL 2026

What we process

When you verify a video, audio clip, or image with Veritas, the media is copied to a private storage area on your device and processed entirely on-device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

What leaves your device

Only two things: (1) detection model updates downloaded on a schedule, and (2) anonymous telemetry, if you have opted in. Model updates are signed and verified before loading.

Data retention

Media is deleted 60 seconds after you see the verdict. Verdict history is stored locally on your device only — we never see it. You can clear it in Settings at any time.

Third parties

None, for your media. Google Play Services is used for app distribution and the LiteRT runtime. Our model CDN delivers signed model updates.

Your rights

We don't hold personal data about you, so there's nothing to request or delete. If you have questions, email privacy@veritas.app.

How Veritas works
01 · DESIGN

Detection is hard, so we're honest.

Most deepfake detectors give you a percentage and a binary verdict. Veritas gives you three: Authentic, Synthetic, or Uncertain — with a confidence range, not a point. If we're not sure, we tell you.

02 · SIGNALS

What our detectors look for.

For video: texture inconsistencies, lip-sync drift, and subtle color pulses from real blood flow (rPPG) that AI faces lack. For audio: vocoder signatures in the frequency domain, and unnaturally regular prosody. For images: Fourier-domain grid patterns from diffusion models, and EXIF anomalies.

03 · PROVENANCE

Content Credentials (C2PA).

Some content is signed at the source — by cameras, editing software, or news agencies — using the C2PA standard. Veritas reads these signatures to verify real content directly, rather than guessing.

04 · DEVICE

Why on-device matters.

Your private content never leaves your phone. Our model runs locally, returns a verdict, and that's it. We have no server that sees your media.

05 · LIMITS

What we can't detect.

Very low-quality or heavily-compressed content often returns Uncertain. Novel generators sometimes slip past until we update our models. Live video and streaming are not supported in this version.
