What Veritas is not
Honest about the limits.
01
Not 100% accurate. Detectors return three verdicts with confidence ranges — Authentic, Synthetic, or Uncertain.

Not a content moderator. We don't report, flag, or block anything on your behalf.

Not a replacement for judgment. Use Veritas as one input. Consider the source and context.
