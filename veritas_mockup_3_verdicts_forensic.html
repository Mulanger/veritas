LIP_SYNC_DRIFT
Lip-sync drift
WHAT IT MEANS
The mouth movements don't line up precisely with the audio. AI-generated videos often have small timing errors — typically 40–150ms — especially at word boundaries.
WHY IT MATTERS
Consistent lip-sync is hard for generative models to get perfectly right. Real humans align mouth shapes to phoneme onset within milliseconds.
COULD CAUSE A FALSE POSITIVE
Poor audio recording, heavy re-compression, videos dubbed into another language, or a bad cellular connection during recording.
Close
